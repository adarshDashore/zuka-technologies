SMART MAILBOX SYSTEM

Delivering mails in Dubai where there are no street addresses, no zip codes or area codes

We chose Dubai for doing product's PoC as the city has no street addresses, no zip codes or area codes, and no doorstep postal delivery service. And yet with a population of 200 million it is one of the biggest cosmopolitan in the world.
