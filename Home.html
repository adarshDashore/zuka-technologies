Welcome to Zuka

Zuka is a word expressing union of kefi which means doing something with energy and enthusiasm and word meraki which means doing something with soul, creativity, and love.

We are a product development company

of dreamers and doers united by a simple belief in creating value for your business by creating value for your customers



We work for

Customer Experience
By focusing on the customer, we design your customer’s journey. While optimising every touch point for convenience and delight
Digital Products
Websites, apps, interfaces, Blockchain, IoT, AI and more – we are full-service product strategy, design and development partner
Innovation and Technology
Creative engineering – we offer innovation and high quality, cost effective solutions on multiple platforms at scale
Data Science
Harnessing the power of data we create actionable insights and deeper connections with your customers
